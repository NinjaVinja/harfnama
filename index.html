How it works

Three simple steps, all inside your browser

1

Upload your document

Drag and drop a PDF, Word (.docx), or plain text file. Harfnama reads the text directly in your browser — nothing is uploaded to a server.

2

Pick a font and paper style

Choose from handwritten fonts like Caveat and Dancing Script, or Urdu fonts like Noto Nastaliq Urdu. Add ruled, grid, or dotted lines, and pick a paper size — A4, Letter, Legal, and more.

3

Export your result

Download as a Word document, PDF, PNG image, or standalone HTML file — with your chosen font and paper lines included.

Frequently asked questions

Is Harfnama really free?

Yes. There is no signup, subscription, or hidden paywall. The site is supported by small, non-intrusive ads.

Does my document get uploaded anywhere?

No. PDF and Word parsing happens entirely inside your browser using JavaScript libraries. Your file never leaves your device.

Can I use my own font?

Yes. Harfnama supports uploading your own .ttf, .otf, .woff, or .woff2 font file, which is then used for both the preview and your exports.

What file formats can I export to?

You can export as a Word document (.docx), PDF, PNG image, standalone HTML file, or plain text (.txt).

Does it support Urdu and right-to-left text?

Yes. Harfnama includes Urdu Nastaliq, Naskh, and Scheherazade fonts, along with a right-to-left text direction toggle.

Do I need to install anything?

No installation is required. Harfnama runs directly in your web browser on desktop or mobile.
